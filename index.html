About Us

At E-Waste Finder, we are dedicated to solving the critical problem of electronic waste disposal. Every year, millions of tons of electronic devices are discarded, posing a significant threat to the environment and human health. We aim to tackle this issue by providing a convenient solution for people to dispose of their e-waste responsibly.

We collect e-waste from individuals and businesses, ensuring that it is properly recycled. Our team works tirelessly to transport these products to authorized recyclers in India who utilize the best practices for e-waste recycling. By doing so, we contribute to the conservation of resources and the reduction of environmental pollution.

Join us in our mission to create a cleaner and more sustainable future. Together, we can make a difference.

What We Offer

  • Free pickup of electronic waste from your location
  • Environmentally responsible disposal of e-waste
  • Collaboration with authorized recyclers in India
  • Transparent and ethical recycling process
  • Contribution to a cleaner and safer environment
